Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Introduction

Welcome to the WhoopGPT Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application.

WhoopGPT is designed to interface with your WHOOP account to provide AI-powered insights about your health and fitness data. We take your privacy seriously and handle your data with utmost care.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide

  • Authentication credentials (WHOOP OAuth tokens) to access your WHOOP account
  • Queries and commands you send to WhoopGPT

Information We Access From WHOOP

  • Recovery data
  • Sleep data
  • Workout data
  • Profile information
  • Cycle information
  • Body measurement data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of WhoopGPT, which includes accessing your WHOOP data to generate insights and answer your questions
  • To authenticate you with the WHOOP API
  • To improve our services based on usage patterns
  • To troubleshoot issues and maintain the application

Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Your WHOOP authentication tokens are stored in an encrypted format
  • Tokens are primarily stored in memory and not persisted to disk
  • We do not log sensitive information such as your full authentication tokens
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessed by:

  • Our application to provide you with the requested services
  • The WHOOP API (to retrieve your fitness data)
  • OpenAI's API (to process your queries and generate responses)

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to revoke authorization at any time by disconnecting WhoopGPT from your WHOOP account
  • The right to request information about how your data is processed

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
